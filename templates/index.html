ThoughtPaths

ThoughtPaths is an interactive prototype designed to support reflective exploration through branching AI responses. Instead of guiding users toward a single answer, it helps surface alternative directions, compare perspectives, and keep inquiry open over time.

The prototype enables:

  • A simple and easy-to-use interface;
  • Generation of multiple AI responses per user input, each offering a distinct perspective;
  • Iterative and reflective exploration through a multi-branch conversation interface;
  • A Journey panel that visualizes the dialogue path and key moments in the interaction;
  • Summary generation across branches for sense-making;
  • Image generation to support exploratory thinking;
  • Export of the session as a .doc file.

This system was developed as part of an academic research project and is structured to support direct review and experimentation.
